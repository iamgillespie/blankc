About BlanKC Guitars

Handmade and homemade: Blankc Guitars are made by a music maker who loves to play and loves to create. With an emphasis on woods and electronics, these guitars will sound great and distinctive. Each guitar is (clearly) made by hand and will feel like your favorite guitar. Made for life's big shows and small talk, sweaty basements and outdoor festivals: Blankc guitars will get the job done and feel great doing it.
